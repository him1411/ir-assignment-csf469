Then Vim will use a mix of tabs and spaces, but typing and will behave like a tab appears every 4 (or 3) characters.Note: Setting \'tabstop\' to any other value than 8 can make your file appear wrong in many places (e.g., when printing it).Set \'tabstop\' and \'shiftwidth\' to whatever you prefer and use \'expandtab\'. This way you will always insert spaces. Only works when using Vim to edit the file.Always set \'tabstop\' and \'shiftwidth\' to the same value, and \'noexpandtab\'.
