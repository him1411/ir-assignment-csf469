But enabling an outside line (called strut) will make that side inflexible/ non-flexible.Enabling the outer left line (left strut) is not equivalent to enabling UIViewAutoresizingFlexibleRightMargin. Instead, UIViewAutoresizingFlexibleRightMargin = on if right strut disabled, off if right strut enabled.It is quite confusing at first, but if you see closely, there is a difference in the springs and struts.
